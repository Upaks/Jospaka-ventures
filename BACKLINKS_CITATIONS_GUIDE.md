# Backlinks & Citations Guide for JVNL

## What Are Backlinks?

Backlinks are links from other websites that point to your website. Search engines see them as "votes of confidence" - the more quality backlinks you have, the more trustworthy your site appears.

### Types of Backlinks:

1. **Natural Backlinks** - Earned organically (most valuable)
   - News articles mentioning your projects
   - Industry publications citing your work
   - Client case studies linking to you

2. **Directory Listings** - Business directories (good for local SEO)
   - Google Business Profile
   - Nigerian business directories
   - Industry-specific directories

3. **Social Proof** - Mentions on platforms
   - LinkedIn company page
   - Professional association websites
   - Trade organization listings

---

## What Are Citations?

Citations are mentions of your business information (name, address, phone number) on other websites, even without a link.

### Why Citations Matter:
- **Local SEO**: Critical for appearing in local search results
- **Trust Signals**: Google trusts businesses listed in multiple places
- **Consistency**: Shows your business is legitimate and established
